Child Care Tax Credit

With Montreal Kindergarten being a fully private childcare service centre, we do not offer $7.00 subsidized space. However, you can take full advantage of the tax credit for childcare expenses if you meet certain basic conditions.To see what the conditions are to claim the refundable tax credit for the 2013 taxation year for childcare expenses,Click here. Once the conditions are met, you can get a refund up to 75% of your child care costs. The percentage of reimbursement is made based on your annual household income.
